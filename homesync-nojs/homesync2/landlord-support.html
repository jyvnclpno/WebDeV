Popular FAQs

How do I set up rent collection through GCash?

Go to Rent & Payments, click Payment Settings, then link your GCash number.

How do I assign a ticket to a technician?

Open the ticket from the Tickets page, click "Assign", and enter the technician's contact.

How do I add a new tenant?

Go to Tenants and click "Add Tenant". Fill in their details and set lease dates.

How do I generate a monthly report?

Go to Reports, click the report type, select your date range, and click Generate.
